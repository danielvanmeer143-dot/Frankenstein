Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley - Frankenstein

Welcome to the mini digital edition of Mary Shelley's Frankenstein. Made as a project for Text as Data, taught at the University of Antwerp.

Welcome to my iteration of the Text as Data assignment. My name is Daniel van Meer and in this assignment we will examine the manuscript of Frankenstein, how they were edited and who did it. First however, lets us take a glimps of the minds behind Frankenstein. The first image is of Percy Shelley, the husband of Mary Shelley, who helped in editing the manuscript. Next to him is a depiction Mary Shelley herself, the author of this book.

The full title of this work is Frankenstein; or, The Modern Prometheus. Although the story was first published in 1818, the making of the book began in 1816 at the suggestion of Lord Byron. It has since grown into one of the most famous English works to date.
